# Release Notes

## v1.0.1

Version Features:

1. Search Functionality: Quickly find episodes by title and description with highlighted keywords, making discovery efficient.
2. Listening List: Queue up your next episodes and auto-download them within your set limit for seamless listening.
3. Listening History: Easily track and revisit episodes you've played, organized by recent listening activity.
4. Show Notes Display: Access detailed episode notes beautifully presented for deeper insights.
5. Episode Filtering: Customize your view with filters for played, downloaded, or all episodes, and sort by release date or duration for a tailored experience.
