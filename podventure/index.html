About Podventure

A better podcast player for podcast listeners. Developed by an indie hacker, also a podcast lover.

Features

  • Search Functionality: Quickly find episodes by title and description with highlighted keywords, making discovery efficient.
  • Listening List: Queue up your next episodes and auto-download them within your set limit for seamless listening.
  • Listening History: Easily track and revisit episodes you've played, organized by recent listening activity.
  • Show Notes Display: Access detailed episode notes beautifully presented for deeper insights.
  • Episode Filtering: Customize your view with filters for played, downloaded, or all episodes, and sort by release date or duration for a tailored experience.
