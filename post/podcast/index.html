Podcast (PODCAST) is a form of audio programs similar to radio stations in ancient early days. Its production cost is lower than video content and higher than text content. Compared to reading text, it is much easier to listen to the podcasts. Compared with watching videos, it is easy for a podcast to get more in -depth and detailed insights.

For me, the place where the podcast is most attractive to me is to accompany and nourish, just like sitting among a group of people, listening to them chatting, you can feel everyone’s voice and laughter through the sound, and feel the atmosphere of the conversation at the time. It is important that you can find a lot of interesting people through a favorite podcast and open a lot of interesting worlds.

P.S. 英文播客

Listening to English podcasts is very good for improving English ability. A suggestion is to listen to the show you are most interested in. It is interesting enough to let you listen all the way. As long as you continue to listen, you will find the effect soon. Another suggestion is to pay attention to understanding the entire context rather than hearing each sentence. It may only be able to understand 30%of the content at first, but it can slowly increase to 80%. After listening to daily English interviews, the reading speed of English books will naturally increase.
