In early July 2020, he got the company’s departure certificate. He left the company’s building with a small yellow car under the sun in July. There were very few people on the road. The roads were hot and sunny. In late June 2021, a new work unit is about to enter the job, just a heavy rain, at this time the weather is cool. One year passed, and the days of not going to work will end, and a simple review is here.

** 1. Childhood and game console **

The start of the three months of the beginning, I realized my dream when I was a child. I bought the Xbox Scorpio game console, a 4K display, and played in a small room.

Can’t go back, the immersion of the game in childhood can’t go back. My favorite age is between the third grade and the first year of high school. Being able to mention interest, even when I arrived at the university, I just played with my roommate several times “The Road to Survival 2”. I can’t accept the big time in the game. I feel boring.

This time, the game is very good, the story is great (“Witcher 3”, “Wild Dart 2”), but I can’t immerse themselves. After confirming this, I sold these game devices again.

In addition to playing games, I also discovered some outdoor groups in Shenzhen, crossed the beaches and hiking mountains, and was deeply convinced by the sea view of South Australia. This is the sea I imagined.

In the rest of the day, I just read the book quietly and borrowed “Little Fire” and “Silent Confession” from the library of Chengdu. The homestay that lives is very quiet. After half a month, people who didn’t speak were quite lonely.

** Four, Dali **

In mid -November, he left Chengdu and reached the long -awaited Dali. After two months of unforgettable two months, it is more interesting and beautiful than I imagined.

At the end of a inn, it was difficult to integrate from the beginning, to the familiar place, and the familiar person. The details have been written in the previous diary.

A special of the Dali community I observed is that the crowd here is very diverse, mainly in middle -aged people. The age of 40 is a very full age. Different people gather together. Just listening to them or her sharing, you can feel very rich things. And unlike the sharing of sitting, there are many creators, brewing, bread, organizational activities, writing public accounts, writing novels, climbing, hiking, and building a house. For example, he can build his own shop by himself, cut wood, nails, and work with the workers’ masters.

Some small shops in Dali also give people a different feeling. The shop is the boss himself. He is not only the boss, but also a member of this community. You can communicate. While he provides you at the same time, he is also a person who is equal to you. In standardized and customer -first chain stores, sometimes people forget this and treat services as machines. How can they relax here?

** The development of industrialization has cultivated a lot of specialized people and improved the efficiency of social production, but I actually feel missing. During the day of work, clicking takeaway, taxi, online shopping, a mobile phone can include many scenes of life to others, but therefore feels like becoming a working machine, and all needs can become a product. I want a more delicate life experience, I want to feel my life. **

But in fact, I am anxious in Dali. When someone asked me what I did, I just said “a programmer” and felt very guilty. I haven’t created anything for this world, and I haven’t proved my ability. What can I do?

** 5. Back to Shenzhen **

After returning to Shenzhen, this anxiety quickly dissipated. Back to the familiar scene again, I know that I will go to work soon, and the rest is what to do in the future. The programming world is very large and there are many directions. After trying a few directions, I decided to go back to the direction of my beginning and make this direction well. The rest is to find a job.

** I saw some new things. I found that like Dali’s “Dali good” public account, Shenzhen also has some public accounts to share local activities. Later, I went to the “Psychiatric Declaration” bookstore, went to the fans, and met friends who could watch movies and chat together.

One day, when I saw a picture of cooking a good friend who met Dali, I suddenly wanted to cook, and I had to live well.

I have always been resistant to the kitchen, and I feel that it is greasy and dirty, and I have hardly cooked since I was small. But suddenly, it started, everything was so natural, do it for yourself, do whatever you want. It can always be eaten, and it is quite fulfilling.

What is worse? It is the issue of money. I also have a resistance to making money. The “Chen Zhiwu Financial Course”, which had been bought a long time ago, has gradually picked up, letting “direct financing”, “indirect financing”, “bond”, “equity” and so on. Life. In April, maybe the time was a bit mature, and I suddenly wanted to pick up the habit of meditation. A few years ago, I started to contact meditation intermittently, and I also knew that it had a lot of benefits, but I couldn’t do it. After repeatedly hearing the guests in Jade Podcast mention meditation, I determined to start, so I started the habit of morning meditation. I think that during the time of finding a job, in the adjustment of mentality, it may also benefit from the habit of meditation. I can reduce some resistance and face emotions.

In June, I voted for my resume, found my favorite job, returned to my professional direction, hoping to do some useful creations.


It’s almost a whole year, is it “Gap Year”? When I chose to start this year, I did not expect that it would develop this way, but many life topics I wanted to solve at that time were answered to a certain extent.
