Day 3 攀岩&徒步

After eating a large bowl of pot of rice noodles in the morning, go to the Baiyan Rock Farm and try climbing. There is already a ready -made hanging point on the rock wall. The coaches climbed up along the hanging point, hung the fulcrum at the highest place, and hung the rope. The fulcrum is a sliding rail structure. One end of the rope is buckled on the climber’s waist, and the other end is buckled on the protector’s waist. As the climbers go up, the protector will close the rope to ensure that the climbers will not fall even if they lose their hands.

The tip of the rock climbing shoe step on the gap in the rock wall, focus on the legs, and support the waist upwards. But I was nervous, and my arms were sore quickly. Everyone is very powerful, especially girls are very smart. Unlike boys, girls will use more flexible postures. One girl climbed all 5 lines.

The weather was fine that day, and the little partner was about to prepare the mountain road discovered by the walk yesterday in the afternoon.
