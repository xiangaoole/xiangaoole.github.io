“[Now young people, why don’t you want to go to work] (https://www.douban.com/note/655911213/)"? In February 2018, after watching the first episode of “The Round Table” “I don’t want to go to work”, Lin An -an office worker who made digital media marketing wrote this text in Douban.

She continued to interview a few friends who did not go to work after get off work, and began the interview project “100 people who did not go to work.” Through the popularity of this topic, Lin An got a lot of media, magazines, and opportunities for business cooperation, which gave her to feed her confidence through writing. At the same time, in order to prevent the instability of text work in early income, she tried to turn her photography hobby into a living method for paying for a contract. Finally, in the summer of 2018, she resigned. For the second month of resignation, her monthly income exceeded the work salary for the first time.

In a blink of an eye, Lin An has entered the 6th year of the freelance profession. By exploring, transforming, and light entrepreneurship, she is now doing the “Liberal Living Room” of a free professional community and has her own entrepreneurial team.

The book “Only Working, No Work” was published in September 2019. It includes the story of 20 people who do not go to work in “100 people who do not go to work”, and of course Lin An’s own story. In this article, I will share the stories of several partners of the digital nomads.

Of course, there are no shortage of anxiety in digital nomad life. Lin An said, “Doing a freelance career, anxiety is your good friend. The best way to relieve anxiety is to do things. Do not be in trouble when you are anxious.” Xixi is easy to be easy Anxious digital nomad, “Editing for a while without contacting me, I started to panic, wondering if she was left out.” I was afraid that the editor would forget her or be replaced by other authors. However, she can retreat after she has a house. In Beijing, there is a house in Beijing. If you ca n’t do free career, you can collect rent. At the end of the story of Xixi, Lin An reminded those who want to not go to work, the destiny has set the scope of our fate, and we must respect the reality.

Lin An was worried that young people would only see the beautiful side of the freelance, naked without preparing, and finally made their situation bad. When she resigned in 2018, she prepared a deposit that could not starve to death in Shanghai for two years without income, and formulated the first three months to realize the balance of revenue and expenditure. Essence

But perhaps, when there is no direction in my heart, it is also a choice with 20,000 yuan to explore yourself and the world bravely like a ball. As Alison said, “No matter which step to the end of the road, there will always be a way.” The external environment will subtly affect the status and goals of people. When I was together, I found that wealthy friends were also anxious, but even her own anxiety of the future became more. “But when I left that environment and was with digital tourists, this group of people gave each other confidence and encouragement to each other, and made me feel that what I was doing can be recognized and more confident and calm.”

Do you do a good job of re -action, or take action first, and constantly adjust your goals in action? This is not a question of who is wrong, nor is it black or white, but the degree of personality tendency. The fourth latitude in the MBTI personality tendency test is to distinguish your way of working? Are you judging tendencies or a person who is aware of? Those who awareness Pure P pay attention to flexibility and adaptability, seek more new ideas and real experiences, and are willing to explore a new travel destination with unknown unknown.

In this journey of life, there is no standard answer. As mentioned in the ball, “life is not track, but the wilderness.” May you play happily in your wilderness and live well, and live up to this life.

Although after growing up, the car has become a commonplace, but I still miss the window that will move. I think that I also install a window that can move myself in the future. Wilderness, not track.
